# Stage 5: MVP Scope

## Purpose
Define the smallest version of the product that delivers the magic moment.
This is the hardest stage — cutting scope requires discipline. Your job is
to help the user be ruthless about what's in and what's out.

## Questions

### Q1: The Must-Haves
Ask via AskUserQuestion:

> "If your product could only do THREE things, what would they be?
> Think about what's absolutely necessary for the magic moment to work."

Options:
- A) Let me list my top 3
- B) Help me prioritize — I have too many features in my head
- C) I think I only need 1-2 things for v1

**If B:** Ask the user to brain-dump every feature they're thinking about.
Then for each one, ask: "Does the magic moment work without this?" If yes,
it's not v1.

### Q2: The Explicit Cuts
Ask via AskUserQuestion:

> "What are you explicitly NOT building in v1? Being clear about what's
> out is as important as what's in."

Options:
- A) Let me list what I'm cutting
- B) Help me figure out what to cut
- C) I think everything I listed is essential

**If C:** Challenge directly: "Every founder thinks that. Let me push on each
feature: [go through the list and ask 'does the magic moment work without
this?' for each one]."

**Perspective injection opportunity:** "A PM would tell you: if your MVP has
more than 3-5 core features, it's not an MVP — it's a v2 disguised as a v1.
The purpose of an MVP isn't to impress. It's to learn."

### Q3: The Timeline
Ask via AskUserQuestion:

> "How long do you want to spend building v1 before you can use it or show
> someone?"

Options:
- A) This weekend (2-3 days)
- B) One week
- C) Two weeks
- D) A month or more

**If D:** "That's a long time for a v1. What could you ship in ONE WEEK that
gives you 80% of the value? Ship that first, then iterate."

## Section Output Format

```markdown
## 5. MVP Scope

**Must-have features (v1):**
1. [Feature] — [why it's essential for the magic moment]
2. [Feature] — [why]
3. [Feature] — [why]

**Explicitly NOT in v1:**
- [Feature] — [why it can wait]
- [Feature] — [why it can wait]

**Target timeline:** [Weekend / 1 week / 2 weeks]

**Success criteria:** [How you'll know v1 is "done enough" to use]
```
